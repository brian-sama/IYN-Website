International Youth Network

The International Youth Network is a project under Terre Des Hommes Schweiz that brings together youth delegates from various countries. The network includes representatives from Zimbabwe, South Africa, Tanzania, Mozambique, Nicaragua, El Salvador and Switzerland.

About IYN

Connecting young leaders worldwide to create lasting impact in their communities

The International Youth Network connects young leaders worldwide, offering mentorship, development pathways, and platforms for lasting impact in their communities.

Our focus areas include leadership development, civic engagement, entrepreneurship, arts and culture, environmental sustainability, and youth wellbeing.

We believe in the power of young people to drive meaningful change and create a more inclusive, sustainable future for all.
