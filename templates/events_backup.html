About IYN

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the impact we're making through youth empowerment

The International Youth Network connects young leaders worldwide, offering mentorship, development pathways, and platforms for lasting impact in their communities.

Our focus areas include leadership development, civic engagement, entrepreneurship, arts and culture, environmental sustainability, and youth wellbeing.

We believe in the power of young people to drive meaningful change and create a more inclusive, sustainable future for all.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our work with youth across the globe

Our Mission

To empower young people with the skills, resources, and networks needed to become effective leaders and change-makers in their communities.

Our Vision

A world where young people are actively engaged in shaping their futures and creating positive social change across all sectors.

Our Approach

Collaborative, youth-led initiatives that combine local action with global perspectives to address pressing challenges.

About IYN

The International Youth Network is a project under Terre Des Hommes Schweiz that brings together youth delegates from various countries.
